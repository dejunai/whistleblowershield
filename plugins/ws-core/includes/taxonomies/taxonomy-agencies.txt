Notes: Operational palette for agency records. Intentionally slimmer than
       statutes; agencies describe subject area, reporting targets, process
       routes, languages, and jurisdiction only. Full doctrinal detail
       belongs on jx-statute, not ws-agency.

TAXONOMY: ws_disclosure_targets
Applies to: ws-agency
Hierarchical: NO — flat list
Description: Reporting target classification for this agency. Tags what kind
             of reporting body this agency represents. Use the most accurate
             single term in most cases; multi-tag only where genuinely dual.
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

agency-federal           Federal Agency
agency-state             State Agency
agency-local             Local / Municipal Agency
law-enforcement          Law Enforcement

legislative-state        State Legislature
court-filing             Court Filing

TAXONOMY: ws_process_type
Applies to: ws-agency
Hierarchical: NO — flat list
Description: Procedural route this agency administers or accepts.
             Tag all that genuinely apply to this agency's intake process.

Description: Languages in which the agency offers services or intake support.

             assumed and should still be tagged. 'additional' is a system
             flag auto-assigned when ws_agency_additional_languages field
             contains a value — do not set manually.
