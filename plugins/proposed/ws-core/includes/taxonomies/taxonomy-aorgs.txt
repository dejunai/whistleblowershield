================================================================================
FILE: taxonomy-aorgs.txt
Scope: ws-assist-org
Source: register-taxonomies.php v3.12.0
Notes: Triage and directory palette for assist-org records. Oriented around
       who the org helps, what they offer, when in the case lifecycle they
       are useful, and where they operate. Full doctrinal detail belongs on
       jx-statute, not ws-assist-org. The 'additional' term in ws_languages
       and the 'additional' term in ws_aorg_service are system flags —
       auto-set by ACF when companion text fields contain a value —
       do not set manually.
================================================================================
