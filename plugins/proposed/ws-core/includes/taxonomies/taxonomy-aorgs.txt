================================================================================
FILE: taxonomy-aorgs.txt
Scope: ws-assist-org
Source: register-taxonomies.php v3.10.0
Notes: Triage and directory palette for assist-org records. Oriented around
       who the org helps, what they offer, when in the case lifecycle they
       are useful, and where they operate. Full doctrinal detail belongs on
       jx-statute, not ws-assist-org. The 'additional' term in ws_aorg_service
       is a system flag — auto-set by ACF on text present in
       additional_services field — do not include in JSON records.
================================================================================

Applies to: ws-assist-org
Hierarchical: YES — use child slugs only
Description: Subject matter areas the organization works in or accepts cases
             for. Tags what kind of wrongdoing or concern the org is relevant
             to. Use all that apply; err toward broader coverage if unclear.
