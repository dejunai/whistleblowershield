## Project Overview

WhistleblowerShield.org is a legal information and research platform
focused specifically on whistleblower protections, laws, retaliation
defense, and disclosure pathways.

The system will evolve into an enterprise-level legal database
covering whistleblower laws across multiple jurisdictions.

The front-end experience prioritizes legal laypersons rather than lawyers.

## System Philosophy

The project aims to bridge the gap between:

- legal scholarship
- investigative journalism
- public accessibility

WhistleblowerShield should act as both:

1. a **legal research archive**
2. a **survival guide for whistleblowers**

## AI Assistance Policy

AI tools are used for:

- documentation generation
- development assistance
- schema design
- architecture planning
- research workflow structuring

must be verified through primary sources.
