# current-proposals.md

## Active Design Tracks

### Jurisdiction Data Relationships (Refinement Layer)

-   Maintain a consistent parent-child relationship model across
    jurisdiction datasets
-   Ensure each dataset record resolves to a single jurisdiction context
-   Support reliable dataset aggregation for rendering and internal use

### Dataset Completeness Visibility

-   Provide a system-level view of dataset coverage across jurisdictions
-   Enable rapid identification of missing or incomplete data groupings
-   Support internal awareness of structural gaps without relying on
    manual review

### Public Guidance Structuring Model

-   Maintain a layered content approach:
    -   plain-language summaries
    -   practical guidance
    -   supporting legal references
-   Structure content around user-driven questions rather than internal
    data structures
-   Preserve separation between internal systems and public-facing
    simplicity

### Editorial Consistency Alignment

-   Ensure internal editorial standards align with actual content
    expectations
-   Maintain consistency between policy, guidance, and implementation
    behavior
-   Support clear, factual legal descriptions without introducing
    ambiguity

## Proposed Extensions

### Foundational Documentation Expansion

-   Define scope and coverage boundaries for the platform
-   Establish a consistent approach to legal information updates
-   Clarify distinction between legal information and legal advice
-   Provide a lightweight contributor onboarding framework
-   Maintain a clear statement of project context and operational model

### Guidance Layer Enhancements

-   Expand structured guidance patterns for different user scenarios
-   Improve clarity of decision points within guidance content
-   Ensure terminology is consistently explained at the point of use

### Navigation and Information Access

-   Further develop question-driven navigation structures
-   Improve pathways between summary content and deeper legal layers
-   Ensure users can move between guidance and source material without
    friction
