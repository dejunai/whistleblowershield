# WhistleblowerShield

WhistleblowerShield is a legal information project focused on organizing and presenting whistleblower laws and protections.

The system is designed to support two primary user scenarios:

- Individuals considering coming forward to report wrongdoing  
- Individuals who have already come forward and may be facing retaliation  

A third audience—researchers, journalists, and legal observers—is also supported, but is not the primary focus.

The project combines a structured legal data system with a public-facing guidance layer, aiming to make complex legal information more accessible without sacrificing accuracy.

---

## Core Concepts

The system is built around a few foundational ideas:

- Legal information is structured, not just written  
- Jurisdiction matters and must be handled explicitly  
- Content must balance clarity with legal accuracy  
- Guidance should be practical and scenario-driven  

These concepts are explored in more detail throughout the documentation.
