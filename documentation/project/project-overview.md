# Project Overview

WhistleblowerShield is a legal information project focused on organizing and presenting whistleblower laws and protections.

The system is designed to make complex legal information more accessible while preserving accuracy and context. It combines structured legal data with a guidance layer intended to help users understand their situation and possible next steps.

---

## Purpose

The project exists to:

- Organize whistleblower-related laws into a structured, queryable system  
- Provide clear, practical guidance based on real-world user scenarios  
- Maintain a balance between accessibility and legal accuracy  
- Preserve traceability between guidance and underlying legal sources  

---

## System Composition

The project is composed of two primary layers:

### Legal Data System

A structured representation of legal information, including:

- jurisdictions  
- statutes and legal sources  
- relationships between legal concepts  

This layer prioritizes consistency, traceability, and internal coherence.

---

### Guidance Layer

A user-facing layer that:

- translates legal information into practical guidance  
- structures content around user scenarios  
- connects high-level explanations to underlying legal sources  

---

## User Personas (Overview)

The system is designed primarily for two types of users:

### Persona A — Pre-Disclosure

Individuals considering whether to report wrongdoing.

Typical concerns include:

- whether their situation qualifies  
- potential risks and protections  
- how to proceed  

---

### Persona B — Post-Disclosure

Individuals who have already reported wrongdoing and may be experiencing retaliation.

Typical concerns include:

- understanding their legal protections  
- identifying possible violations  
- determining next steps  

---

### Persona C — Research / Secondary

Researchers, journalists, and others seeking structured legal information.

This group is supported, but is not the primary focus of the system.

---

## Development Context: Use of AI Tools

This project is developed with the assistance of AI tools.

AI is used to:

- assist with structuring and refining documentation  
- support analysis and iteration on system design  
- help identify inconsistencies and gaps  

All outputs are reviewed and directed manually. The system design and final decisions remain human-driven.

---

## Project Characteristics

- Iterative and evolving  
- Not fully formalized or fixed  
- Designed to be refined over time  

The documentation reflects current understanding and may change as the system develops.
